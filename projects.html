UAE Cultural Heritage via Generative AI

Observing and preserving UAE cultural heritage through generative AI and immersive technologies — the current flagship HCT Applied Research Grant, uniting digital heritage and emerging AI pipelines.

2009 – 2020 · Digital Heritage

A Famosa Fortress Reconstruction

A long-running programme reconstructing Malaysia's A Famosa Fortress in 3D from textual and visual evidence — extending to AR navigation and mobile game-based learning (EuroMed / Springer, ACM Multimedia).

2019 – 2022 · VR / 3D

Sacred Architecture in VR

Photorealistic 3D and virtual-reality reconstructions of the Prophet Muhammad Mosque and the Prophet Mosque, addressing rendering fidelity and respectful digital representation of sacred sites.

2024 – 2025 · Generative AI

AI Animation Frameworks

A body of work evaluating and structuring generative-AI animation: the PRIDE photorealism framework, a principle-based evaluation of AI animation, and a seamless 2D-to-3D AI-augmented production workflow.

2023 – Present · Training & Consultancy

AI4LIFE

Founder-led initiative delivering 50+ generative-AI training sessions worldwide to 5,000+ professionals across academia, government and industry, with tailored ChatGPT programmes and adoption roadmaps.

2012 · EU-funded · Top 3

VR Heritage Village, Italy

A top-three research proposal for the EU-funded Virtual Reality Heritage Village project in Italy, recognised with the King's College London / EU Virtual Heritage Village award.
