The Programme

AI4LIFE

Founded and led by Dr. Mohamad Izani, AI4LIFE (Artificial Intelligence for Life) has delivered 50+ training sessions worldwide, empowering more than 5,000 professionals across academia, corporate and public-sector organisations. Sessions pair clear conceptual grounding with interactive workshops and hands-on labs, so participants leave able to apply generative AI to real tasks.

Engagements range from single keynotes and half-day workshops to multi-session curricula and organisation-wide adoption roadmaps — tailored to the audience, whether educators, executives, designers or technical teams.
